Services

We offer exceptional service for fitness and wellness in Athens, Greece.

Elite training

Under the coaching of Christos Papachristos and his assistants, elite and emerging elite Track & Field athletes will join a team that offers an uncompromising training environment.

Kids Training

Training plan to introduce school students to track and field and help them develop athletic skills for success in the sport. Safety and effective instruction, that promotes love of the sport and motivates young athletes to come out year after year.

Recreation Sport

Individual daily program.

Group-Company training

Papachristos Running Team is open to every ability level. Our mission to get more people outside, run and above all have as much fun as possible.

Personal Training

PRT is committed to offering training opportunities to assist you in achieving your personal goals by customizing your sessions.
